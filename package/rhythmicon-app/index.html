A universe of rhythms

The rhythmicon allows to browse, analyze, and experiment with rhythmic patterns (or just rhythms).

A rhythm is a repeated sequence of beats and rests in a steady pulse. Popular examples include two beats in four pulses (as downbeat x-x- or as upbeat -x-x-) and three beats in a pulse of eight (x--x--x-).
