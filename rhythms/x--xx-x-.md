Played as `|D--kD-T-|` on Darbuka in Arabian music.

Written in 2/4 meter

Source: <https://web.archive.org/web/20141026164509/http://www.maqamworld.com/rhythms/muwashahat1.html#ayyub>

It's a Tresillo `|x--x--x-|` with the additional second beat.
